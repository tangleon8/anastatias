About Anastasia’s Light

Honoring a daughter. Lifting our community.

We raise funds and awareness for mental health nonprofits through community events and partnerships.

Founded in loving memory of Anastasia, Anastasia’s Light is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit committed to shining hope on mental health. We collaborate with trusted organizations that provide therapy, crisis support, and equine-assisted services.

Every donation and event ticket helps expand access to compassionate care. Together, we can bring light to those navigating darkness and build resilient, healthy communities.

Anastasia’s Light Incorporated is a 501(c)(3) public charity. Contributions may be tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.
